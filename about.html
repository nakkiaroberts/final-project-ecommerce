About Us

Mission Statement

With a thoughtful and passionate vision, Bayleaf is a bootstrap initiative to bring quality fabrics to our national family with the goal of bringing our passion and vision to nations around the world.

By being agents of change, we offer an inexpensive and eco-friendly expression of our way of doing business for our beautiful planet and humanity.

History

The vision began September 2022. Spread across the United States in three different directions, the founders toiled and wrestled with how to bring what would come to be called, Bayleaf, to fruition. Carried by an enduring spirit of persistence and future orientation, the trio threaded together what they hope to be a global household name.
